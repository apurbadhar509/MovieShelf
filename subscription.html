Frequently Asked Questions

Can I cancel anytime? +

Yes. There are no contracts or commitments. You can cancel your subscription at any time, and you will not be charged after your current billing period ends.

What is a "Premium" movie? +

Premium movies are our latest releases and exclusive titles, available only to Standard and Premium plan subscribers. They are marked with a gold PREMIUM badge on the poster.

How many screens can I watch on? +

Basic: 1 screen. Standard: 2 screens simultaneously. Premium: 4 screens simultaneously.

Is this a real payment? +

No — this is a student project demo. No real payment is processed. Clicking "Subscribe" simply updates your account status in the database for demonstration purposes.
